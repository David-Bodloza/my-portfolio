My Journey: From Challenges to Code

At 29, with a daughter who is my world, I've faced my share of life's complex challenges. Living with my parents and navigating personal complexities, there was a point where I felt truly low. But that feeling fueled a powerful resolve: to rise, to build a better future, and to prove what I'm capable of. That resolve led me to the transformative world of web development.

I dove into learning with an intensity born of necessity and passion. Each line of code, every problem solved, has been a step forward, not just in my career, but in my life. This journey is about resilience, continuous learning, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

I'm now emerging as a dedicated **Full Stack Web Developer**, ready to apply my skills to real-world challenges and contribute meaningfully to a team. The Christian Refugees Aid website project was my first significant step, demonstrating my ability to deliver a complex, functional, and visually appealing solution. I'm eager to bring this same dedication, problem-solving ability, and drive to your organization.
